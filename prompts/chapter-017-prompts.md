# Chapter 17 Image Prompts

## Canon + Style Lock
- Use the **original established story look** from the strongest early Chapter 1-era favorite images: stylized 3D animated storybook-film frame, soft cinematic lighting, rounded but expressive faces, detailed environments, adventurous family-film tone.
- Do **not** drift into generic Pixar parody, anime, flat illustration, photorealism, or random redesigns.
- Keep character faces, ages, and silhouettes consistent across pages.
- **Julian:** 7, sandy blonde hair, hazel-blue eyes, no freckles, alert planner energy, notebook frequently present.
- **Ezra:** 5, dark brown hair, brown eyes, freckles, soft round face, orange hoodie or simple colony clothes, **gold heart-shaped shard glow centered in chest**.
- **Declan:** 2, light brown wispy hair, brown eyes, no freckles, green jumpsuit or toddler colony clothes, tiny and sturdy, fearless expression, **no fragment and no personal glow**.
- **Ollie:** medium grey-silver schnoodle, curly coat, very expressive eyes.
- **Sable:** 15, tan skin, dark hair pulled back, dark eyes, thin jaw scar, practical dark traveler clothes, silver-blue pendant fragment.
- **Rafe:** 12, red hair, pale skin, dark freckles, dark eyes, quiet resilient expression, amber-orange pendant fragment.
- **Petra:** 13, short red hair close-cropped at the sides, weather-tanned pale skin, practical worn clothes, iridescent violet-white pendant fragment.
- **Noa:** 12, dark skin, close-cropped natural hair, big headphones around neck, patched oversized tan cargo jacket, yellow-green teardrop shard on woven-wire wrist cord.
- Fragment colors must stay distinct and attached only to holders.
- Treat magic/power like part of the world, not a different genre.
