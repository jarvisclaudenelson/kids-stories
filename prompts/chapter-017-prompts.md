# Chapter 17 Image Prompts

## Style Bible — Match the Original Favorite Image
Use the user-provided favorite image as the north star for **all** Chapter 17 pages.

### Non-negotiable style targets
- 3D animated storybook / family sci-fi adventure frame
- Match the **original existing character style**, not generic Pixar drift
- Slightly rounded child face shapes, expressive large eyes, soft cheeks, readable silhouettes
- Warm cinematic lighting with dramatic highlights and controlled contrast
- Rich but grounded color palette; not hyper-saturated toy colors
- Detailed environments, but characters stay the focus
- Hair should feel sculpted/stylized and consistent across pages
- Materials should feel soft-real: fabric, brushed metal, plastic, fur
- Emotional storytelling first; avoid poster-like posing unless scene calls for it

### Avoid
- Generic Pixar look that changes face shapes too much
- Over-aged kids, teenified faces, or babyified faces
- Anime eyes, hyperreal skin, glossy plastic faces
- Random costume changes
- Inconsistent shard colors or shapes
- Declan accidentally glowing like a fragment holder

### Character lock
- **Julian:** 7, sandy blonde hair, hazel-blue eyes, no freckles, sharp planner energy, notebook often present
- **Ezra:** 5, dark brown hair, brown eyes, freckles, gentle rounder face, orange hoodie or practical colony clothes, **gold heart-shaped shard in chest**
- **Declan:** 2, light brown wispy hair, brown eyes, green jumpsuit / toddler colony clothes, tiny and sturdy, **no fragment and no personal glow**
- **Ollie:** medium schnoodle, grey-silver curly coat, expressive intelligent eyes
- **Sable:** 15, tan skin, dark hair pulled back, dark eyes, thin jaw scar, practical traveler clothes, silver-blue pendant
- **Rafe:** 12, red hair, pale skin, dark freckles, quiet expression, amber pendant
- **Petra:** 13, short red hair close-cropped at sides, weather-tanned pale skin, practical worn clothes, iridescent violet-white pendant
- **Noa:** 12, dark skin, close-cropped natural hair, big headphones around neck, patched oversized tan cargo jacket with rolled sleeves, practical boots, yellow-green teardrop shard on woven-wire wrist cord
