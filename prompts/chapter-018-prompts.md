# Chapter 18 Image Prompts

## Canon + Style Lock
- Use the **original established story look** from the strongest early favorite images: stylized 3D animated storybook-film frame, soft cinematic lighting, rounded expressive faces, readable environments, emotional but grounded adventure tone.
- Avoid drift into generic Pixar clone, anime, flat illustration, photoreal humans, or random redesigns.
- Character continuity is mandatory.
- **Julian:** 7, sandy blonde hair, hazel-blue eyes, no freckles, notebook always nearby, intense analytical expressions.
- **Ezra:** 5, dark brown hair, brown eyes, freckles, orange hoodie or simple colony clothes, **gold heart-shaped shard glow in chest** unless specifically shifting during the Chapter 18 transformation beat.

- **Ollie:** medium grey-silver schnoodle, curly coat, expressive intelligent eyes.
- **Sable:** 15, tan skin, dark hair pulled back, jaw scar, silver-blue pendant.
- **Rafe:** 12, red hair, pale skin, dark freckles, amber-orange pendant.

- **Captain Okonkwo:** compact, authoritative adult woman with close-cropped silver-gray locs or hair, dark colony uniform/jacket, stillness and command.
- Use fragment colors carefully and only on holders.
