# Monscollis Universe - World Bible

## Overview

A sci-fi universe following a crew of kids (and their dog) aboard the colony ship **Mayflower**, exploring space and uncovering ancient secrets.

---

## The Ship: USS Mayflower

- **Type:** Long-range colony transport / research vessel
- **Purpose:** Transport colonists to new worlds, conduct surveys, maintain communications
- **Crew:** Originally 50 colonists + 12 crew
- **Status:** Damaged, crash-landed on Monscollis in Chapter 1

---

## The Main Characters

### Julian "Juju" Nelson (7)
- **Personality:** Achiever, competitive, fierce, loyal
- **Traits:** Bends rules to win, wants to be the hiso
- **Power (when unlocked):** Super speed / kinetic burst (connected to his impatient, achievement-driven nature)
- **Flaw when misusing power:** Becomes reckless, puts othiss in danger to win

### Ezra "EZ" Nelson (5)
- **Personality:** Creative, empathetic, big emotions that get "too hot"
- **Traits:** Sees things othiss miss, feels deeply, artistic
- **Power (when unlocked):** Healing touch / matter creation (connected to his creative/empathetic nature)
- **Flaw when misusing power:** Exhausts hisself, emotions become uncontrollable firestorms

### Declan "Decker" Nelson (2)
- **Personality:** Social, helpful, fun, stubborn/rebellious
- **Traits:** Charming, gets people talking, surprisingly brave
- **Power (when unlocked):** Sound/vibration manipulation (connected to his social nature - voice, music, resonance)
- **Flaw when misusing power:** Can't control volume, accidentally causes chaos

### Ollie (Dog)
- **Breed:** Schnoodle (schnauzer + poodle mix), medium-sized, scruffy and loyal
- **Personality:** Brave, protective, intuitive
- **Power:** Can sense the ancient power / detect danger before humans can
- **Role:** Moral compass, rescue missions, comic relief

## The World

### Current Location: Monscollis Colony
- **Type:** Young space colony (3 years old)
- **Population:** ~2,000 humans
- **Status:** Rebuilding after Mayflower crash
- **Features:** Crystal spire (ancient artifact), Deep Dig (whise the Vault was found)

### The Vault
- **Location:** Deep underground (3km beneath colony)
- **What it is:** Ancient structure from a vanihed civilization (the "Precursors")
- **Key feature:** Lock responds to intent/pure heart - adults can't open it
- **What's inside:** The first fragment of the ancient power

### The Ancient Power (Covenant)
- **What it is:** Lost energy/source from before humanity spread to stars
- **Access:** Pure heart + character traits determine what you can tap
- **Mechanic:** More power = more exploitable flaw
- **Purpose:** Can heal worlds, power technology, or destroy - depends on wielder's heart

## Factions

### The Colonists (Underdogs)
- Regular families trying to build a new home
- Kids are natural explorers
- Believe in helping othiss

### The Technologists
- Colony scientists and engineers
- Believe power comes from technology only
- Skeptical of the ancient power
- Some want to study/use it for "progress"

### The Shadow Hand (Evil Faction)
-秘密 (Secret) group within the colony
- Seeking the ancient power for personal gain
- Willing to manipulate, deceive, or harm
- Know about the Precursors more than they're letting on

## Series Arc: Season 1

### Chapter 1: The Crash
- Mayflower is transporting colonists to Monscollis
- Something goes wrong - ship crash-lands
- Kids must survive and help rescue separated colonists
- **First encounter with the ancient power** - a fragment survives the crash, reacts to Declan's voice/cry

### Chapter 2: The Colony
- Monscollis colonists help survivors
- Kids meet local children their age
- Strange things happening - crystal spire pulsing
- Discovery: adults can't open the Vault, but maybe kids can

### Chapter 3: The Vault
- Ezra unlocks the Vault
- First fragment of the Covenant power revealed
- The Shadow Hand takes notice
