## Image Generation Prompt Template

```
Pixar-style 3D animated scene of [describe the scene and action], 
including CHARACTERS: 
- Julian: 7-year-old Caucasian boy, sandy blonde short hair, blue/hazel eyes, brave expression
- Ezra: 5-year-old Caucasian boy, dark brown medium-length hair, brown eyes, curious expression
- Declan: 2-year-old Caucasian boy, medium brown short hair, brown eyes, toddler
- Ollie: medium-sized scruffy Schnoodle (schnauzer+poodle mix), white and gray curly fur, loyal expression

[Additional scene details...]
Disney Pixar quality, adventure movie poster style, warm lighting
```

---

## Image Generation Frequency

- **Every 2-3 pages** of story content
- Create 3-5 illustrations per chapter depending on chapter length
- Key moments to capture:
  - Opening scene / establishing shot
  - Major action moments
  - Emotional moments
  - Cliffhanger / chapter end

---

## Generation Settings

- **Model:** DALL-E 3
- **Size:** 1792x1024 (landscape)
- **Style:** vivid (for dramatic, colorful illustrations)
- **Output:** Save to `public/images/chapter{N}/` in kids-stories-reader
