# Chapter 14: The Final Search

The team returned to Monscollis with five fragments. Everyone was proud of them. 

But Captain Okonkwo had some bad news. 

"The Shadow Hand has found the last two fragments," she said. 

Ezra gasped. "Where are they?"

"They are keeping them in a secret base on the other side of the ridge," the Captain explained. "They want to break them tonight!"

---

"We have to stop them!" Ezra shouted. 

"But how?" Julian asked. "They have lots of guards and big machines."

"We have something they don't," Sable said. "We have each other. And we have our powers."

The kids decided to train together one last time. They went to their secret clearing in the forest. 

Ollie kept a sharp eye out for any "coldie mans." 

---

First, Ezra practiced his stretching. He could now reach twelve feet! 

Then Rafe showed off his telekinesis. He lifted three heavy rocks at the same time. 

Petra used her purple-white light to make a bright shield. 

"This can protect us from the bad guys," she said. 

Even Noa found her power. She could make the yellow-green light pulse in a way that made everyone feel fast and strong. 

"I call it 'The Boost'!" Noa laughed. 

---

Julian was busy writing a special plan in his notebook. 

"Okay, here's what we do," Julian said. "Ezra will stretch to unlock the back door. Petra will use her shield to keep us safe. Rafe will move the guards out of the way. And Noa will give us the boost to run fast!"

"What about me?" Julian asked with a grin. 

"You're the leader, Julian," Ezra said. "You'll tell us when to go!"

Julian looked very happy. He was finally the hero he wanted to be. 

---

Later that evening, Declan was playing with Rafe's telekinesis stone. 

"Boo!" Declan said, and the stone gave a tiny wiggle. 

"Whoa!" Rafe said. "Did you see that? Declan is starting to use his power too!"

"Decker is very fast," Ezra said. 

Declan patted Ezra's hand. "You go. I stay. Tell lone-ee."

Declan knew that his brother had a big mission. He was staying home to keep Mom and Dad safe. 

---

As the sun began to set, the team got ready. 

They wore dark clothes to hide in the shadows. They each had their glowing fragment tucked safely in their pockets. 

"Are you ready?" Sable asked. 

Ezra looked at his friends. He saw Julian, Rafe, Petra, and Noa. He saw his brave dog, Ollie. 

"We're ready," Ezra said. 

They started walking toward the Shadow Hand's base. It was time for the final rescue.
