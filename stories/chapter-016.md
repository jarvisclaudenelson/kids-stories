# Chapter 16: The Covenant Reborn

The team reached the crystal spire just as the moons were highest in the sky. 

The spire was waiting for them. It gave off a soft, slow pulse that felt like a giant's heartbeat. 

"Okay, everyone," Sable said. "We have to put the fragments into the slots at the base."

There were seven small holes in the white stone. 

"I'll go first," Ezra said. He placed his golden star into the middle hole. 

One by one, the others followed. 

---

Blue. Orange. Purple. Green. Red. Silver. 

When the last fragment was in place, the ground began to shake — but it was a happy shake. 

Suddenly, a giant beam of light shot out from the top of the spire. It was every color of the rainbow mixed together. 

The beam went high into the clouds, lighting up the whole planet. 

"Look!" Julian pointed. 

Across the ridge, the Shadow Hand's dark base flickered and turned off. Without the fragments, their machines had no power. 

---

The light from the spire was so bright that even the people in the colony woke up. They came out of their houses and cheered when they saw the beautiful rainbow in the sky. 

Captain Okonkwo and Marcus arrived at the spire. 

"You did it," the Captain said with a proud smile. "You saved the Covenant."

"We saved each other," Ezra said. 

The spire gave one final, giant pulse. The seven fragments were now part of the spire forever. They would keep the world safe and warm. 

---

The next day, there was a big party in the colony. 

There were balloons, music, and lots of delicious food. Even Ollie got a special dog treat. 

Julian, Ezra, Rafe, Petra, and Noa were given special medals. 

"You are the Guardians of the Covenant," Captain Okonkwo announced to the crowd. 

Julian looked at his medal and grinned. "I finally became a hero," he whispered to Ezra. 

"We all did," Ezra said. 

---

Later that evening, the family was back at their cabin. 

Declan was sitting on Ezra's lap, looking at the glowing spire in the distance. 

"All done?" Declan asked. 

"All done, Decker," Ezra said. 

"Good," Declan said. He patted Ezra's hand. "No more lone-ee."

Ezra felt a warm, fuzzy feeling in his heart. It was even better than the glowing fragment. 

---

Rafe, Petra, and Noa were staying in a new building next to the cabin. They were like part of the Nelson family now. 

"Will we have another adventure?" Rafe asked as they sat on the porch. 

"Probably," Sable said with a wink. "The universe is a very big place."

"And we have the best team to explore it," Julian added, opening a new page in his notebook. 

---

As the stars came out, Ezra looked up at the sky. 

He wasn't just a boy who crashed on a planet anymore. He was a Guardian. 

The crystal spire pulsed once, soft and slow. It was a heartbeat of peace. 

Ezra closed his eyes and smiled. Everything was going to be okay. 

**THE END**
