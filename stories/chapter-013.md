# Chapter 13: The Fifth Fragment

The *Ariadne* flew to a place called Meridian Point. It wasn't a planet. It was a giant space station made of metal boxes bolted together. 

"Wow!" Ezra said. "Look at all the ships!"

There were ships of every shape and size. Some looked like triangles, and others looked like giant bubbles. 

"We need to find a girl named Noa," Sable said. "She has the yellow-green fragment."

Julian looked at his notebook. "She is at a coffee shop in the middle of the station!"

---

The space station was busy and loud. There were robots rolling around and people talking in different languages. 

"Stay close," Marcus warned. 

They found the coffee shop. A girl with dark skin and a big jacket was sitting at a table. She had giant headphones around her neck. 

"Are you Noa?" Ezra asked quietly. 

The girl looked up. She looked at Ezra's star, then at the others. 

"I've been waiting for you," she said. She showed them her wrist. A yellow-green stone was tied to it with a string. 

---

Suddenly, two men in grey suits appeared at the door. It was the Shadow Hand!

"They're here!" Julian whispered. 

"Quick, follow me!" Noa said. She knew all the secret paths in the station. 

They ran through a narrow hallway full of steam pipes. Then they hid behind a pile of large cargo crates. 

Ezra held his breath. He could hear the heavy boots of the agents walking past. 

"They're gone," Noa whispered after a minute. 

---

They made it back to the *Ariadne* just in time. 

"That was a close call," Ezra said, sitting down. 

Noa looked at the other kids. "I thought I was all alone," she said. "I found my fragment in a box, and it's been my only friend for a long time."

"You have lots of friends now," Rafe said. 

Ezra showed her Declan's special rock. "Declan sent this for you," he said. 

Noa took the rock and held it tight. "Thank you," she said softly. 

---

When all five fragments came together, the ship's cabin filled with light. 

Gold, blue, orange, purple, and yellow-green. It was the most beautiful thing Ezra had ever seen. 

"We have five!" Julian shouted. "That means we have reached the limit! The Shadow Hand can't break them now!"

"Not unless they take them from us," Sable reminded him. 

But Ezra wasn't worried. He felt a strong connection to his new friends. 

---

Ollie the dog walked over to Noa. He put his head on her knee. 

"I've never had a dog before," Noa said, petting his scruffy grey fur. 

"Ollie likes you," Ezra said. "He knows you have a good heart."

Noa smiled. She felt safe for the first time in over a year. 

Wren the pilot started the engines. "Next stop, Monscollis," she said. 

They were going home. 

---

That night, everyone was happy. They had five fragments and five friends. 

Julian was busy writing everything down. 

Rafe and Petra were showing Noa how they could use their powers. 

Ezra looked out at the dark space. He knew there were only two fragments left to find. 

"We are going to save the Covenant," Ezra said. 

"One step at a time," Sable said, patting his shoulder. 

Ezra closed his eyes and fell asleep to the soft hum of the spaceship.
