# Chapter 12: The Race for the Fourth

Julian found it! He figured out the pattern in his notebook. 

"The next fragment is on a planet called Gerald!" Julian shouted. 

"Gerald?" Ezra asked. "That's a funny name for a planet."

"I named it that," Julian said with a shrug. "It's a strong name."

Sable checked her star map. "He's right. There is a signal coming from that system. We have to go now before the Shadow Hand gets there!"

---

They all hopped back on the *Ariadne*. 

"Fasten your seatbelts," Wren the pilot said. "It's going to be a bumpy ride."

The ship zoomed through space. Ezra, Rafe, and Sable sat together. Their fragments were pulsing fast. They were excited to meet a new friend. 

"I have a piece of toast for the new person," Declan said. He had a crumpled piece of toast in his pocket. 

"Good idea, Decker," Ezra said. 

---

Planet Gerald was a rocky place with four small moons. It looked very cold. 

When they landed, they saw a girl running toward them. She had short red hair and a tough look on her face. 

Behind her was a man in a grey suit. It was a Shadow Hand agent!

"Hey!" Ezra yelled. "Leave her alone!"

The girl stopped and turned around. She looked very angry. 

---

Suddenly, the girl's hands started to glow with a bright purple-white light. 

"Get back!" she shouted at the bad guy. 

A beam of light shot out from her hands. It was so bright that the man had to cover his eyes. He turned around and ran away as fast as he could. 

"Wow!" Julian said. "That was amazing!"

The girl looked at them. She was still holding her glowing violet stone. 

"Who are you people?" she asked. 

---

"I am Ezra," Ezra said, showing his golden star. "And we are here to help."

The girl's name was Petra. She had been living on Gerald with her uncle, but she got lost when the Shadow Hand arrived. 

"I've been alone for four months," Petra said. She looked at Ezra's fragment. "I thought I was the only one."

"You're not," Rafe said. He showed her his orange stone. 

Petra smiled. She looked like she was glad to finally have some friends. 

---

Declan walked up to Petra. He reached into his pocket and pulled out the toast. 

"For you," Declan said. 

Petra took the toast and ate a big bite. "Thanks, kid. I was really hungry."

Ezra felt a warm glow in his chest. Now they had four fragments. 

When they all stood together, the lights (gold, blue, orange, and purple) made a beautiful rainbow. 

"We are winning!" Julian cheered, writing "PETRA" in his notebook. 

---

They all got back on the ship. 

"Where to next?" Petra asked. She was already feeling like part of the team. 

"We need to find the last three," Sable said. "But the Shadow Hand is getting desperate. We have to be careful."

Ezra looked at his new friends. They were all different, but they all had brave hearts. 

"We can do it," Ezra said. 

As the *Ariadne* flew back to the stars, Ezra knew that their team was getting stronger every day.
