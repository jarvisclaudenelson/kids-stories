# Chapter 15: Into the Shadow's Den

The Shadow Hand base was a giant, dark building made of black metal. It was hidden behind a tall cliff. 

"Look at the guards," Julian whispered. There were men in grey suits everywhere. 

"We can do this," Ezra said. 

Following Julian's plan, Ezra stretched his arm way up high. He reached a small window and unlocked it. 

One by one, the kids climbed inside. Even Ollie was quiet as he hopped through the window. 

---

Inside, it was cold and dark. There were big machines that made a scary *thump-thump* sound. 

Suddenly, a giant searchlight started to swing toward them. 

"Shield!" Julian hissed. 

Petra stepped forward. She held out her hands, and a bright purple-white wall appeared. The searchlight hit the shield and bounced away. The guards didn't see a thing!

"Good job, Petra," Ezra whispered. 

---

They reached a large room with a glass case in the middle. Inside the case were the last two fragments. One was bright red and the other was shiny silver. 

"There they are!" Julian said. 

But three guards were standing in the way. 

"I've got this," Rafe said. He focused on two heavy metal crates near the door. 

The crates lifted into the air and slid right in front of the guards. 

"Hey! What's happening?" the guards shouted, but they were trapped!

---

Ezra ran to the glass case. He reached out with his fragment, and the glass melted away like ice in the sun. 

He grabbed the red and silver stones. 

"I have them!" Ezra yelled. 

But just then, the lights in the room got very bright. A man in a long black coat stepped out from behind a machine. It was the leader of the Shadow Hand!

"Give those back!" the leader shouted. 

---

"No way!" Ezra said. 

The leader reached out to grab Ezra, but Noa stepped forward. 

"Boost!" Noa yelled. Her yellow-green fragment pulsed with a powerful light. 

Suddenly, Ezra and the others felt like they had lightning in their feet. They were faster than they had ever been!

"Run!" Julian commanded. 

They zoomed past the leader and out the back door before he could even blink. 

---

They didn't stop running until they reached the forest. 

"We did it!" Julian panted. "We have all seven!"

Ezra opened his hands. He had the gold, blue, orange, purple, green, red, and silver stones. 

They were all glowing so brightly that the forest looked like it was in the middle of a beautiful dream. 

"Now what?" Petra asked. 

"Now we go to the crystal spire," Sable said. "It's time to bring the Covenant back to life."

---

The kids looked at each other and smiled. They were tired, but they were very happy. 

They had faced the Shadow Hand and won. 

"Let's go home," Ezra said. 

Ollie wagged his tail and led the way through the trees. The stars above seemed to be cheering for them as they marched back to the colony.
